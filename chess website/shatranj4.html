Shatranj 4.0

Greetings Everyone !!!

The 4th Shatranj Open Prize Money Lichess Championship 2021 was successfully organized by the AEC CHESS CLUB on 22nd May, 2021. The event went on smoothly and we present our vote of thanks to every participant for giving their time and support. This time, we got many players from Assam as well as from outside the state. A Fide Master (FM) ranked player also participated in the event !

We would like to give special credits to Mr. Ravikant Tiwari ( Chief Arbiter) and Mr. Ashok Baruah (Teacher-in-charge, AEC CHESS CLUB) for their unconditional support and help in organizing this tournament.

A total of 141 players participated in the 4th Shatranj Open Prize Money Lichess Championship 2021, which is the highest number of participants in any of the Shatranj editions !
